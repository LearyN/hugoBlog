# Privacy Policy for ADs-LearyClaw

*Last updated: July 9, 2026*

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how ADs-LearyClaw ("we", "our", or "the Application") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our Google Ads management service.

## Information We Collect

When you authorize ADs-LearyClaw through Google OAuth, we may access:

- **Google Ads account data**: campaign performance metrics, ad group data, keyword statistics, search term reports, and other advertising-related information
- **Google Account information**: your name and email address, used solely for API authentication

## How We Use Information

All accessed data is used exclusively for:

- Analyzing and optimizing your Google Ads campaigns
- Generating performance reports and actionable insights
- Executing campaign management operations you explicitly request

## Data Storage and Security

- We do **not** store your Google Ads data on our servers long-term
- Data is processed in real-time and only retained temporarily for analysis sessions
- All API communications are encrypted via HTTPS
- OAuth 2.0 tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time

## Data Sharing

We **do not** sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Access to your Google Ads account is used solely within the scope of your explicit requests.

## Your Rights

You can revoke ADs-LearyClaw's access to your Google account at any time via:
- Your [Google Account permissions page](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
- Your Google Ads API Center
